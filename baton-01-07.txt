  topology:

                            ‚Ēú‚ĒÄ logical   ‚ĒÄ‚Ēľ‚ĒÄ synthesize ‚ĒÄ(route)‚ĒÄ‚Ē¨‚ĒÄ think

  - surface ‚ÄĒ emits the final answer (in Helix, with the humanize rewrite). Only
  this node's tokens stream to the user.
  - remember / breathe / steer ‚ÄĒ store insights, recover "energy," and the
  human-in-the-loop pause (interrupt_before=["steer"]).
